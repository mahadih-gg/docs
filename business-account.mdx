## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What forms of payment can you help me accept?">
    We offer a variety of payment options ranging from **DafriPremier™**, **cards**, **instant EFT**, to **crypto e-commerce**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does the application process take, and what is required?">
    Upon proper completion of the application, your **DafriPremier™ account is approved automatically**. However, **credit card approval** can be immediate or take from **one to five business days** depending on several factors including industry, credit history, and responsiveness.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my money after getting paid via DafriPremier™?">
    You will receive the funds into your **DafriPremier™ Account instantly**. If **Auto-Settlement** is activated, funds will be redirected to your **nominated local bank account**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What is the price of having a Business Account with DafriPremier™?">
    We are **100% transparent** on our pricing. You **don't pay** to have a **Business Account**. However, if you wish to have an **additional Dollar Account**, the fee is **as little as &dollar;2.99 per month**, payable via DBA.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What is a chargeback?">
    A **chargeback** is a **charge reversal** initiated by the cardholder's **bank on behalf of the cardholder**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
